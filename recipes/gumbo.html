Gumbo

"Gumbo is a stew popular in the U.S. state of Louisiana, and is the official state cuisine. Gumbo consists primarily of a strongly-flavored stock, meat or shellfish, a thickener, and the Creole "holy trinity" ― celery, bell peppers, and onions. Gumbo is often categorized by the type of thickener used, whether okra or filé powder (dried and ground sassafras leaves)..."

Continue reading on Wikipedia.

Ingredients

  • Holy Trinity
  • Prepared dark chocolate colored roux.
  • 2 chicken thighs cubed
  • 1/2 pound andouille sliced
  • 6 okra sliced

Steps

  1. Brown meat and okra in pan with veg. oil.
  2. Reserve meat and okra, get roux to shimmering.
  3. Add holy trinity to roux, cooking 15 minutes.
  4. Add meat/okra back to pan, stir and incorporate chicken broth.
  5. Cook under veggies tender & thickened.
